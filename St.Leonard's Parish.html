About Us

Hello! Welcome to St.Leonard's Parish in Brampton. We hope you have a great day! Our patron saint, St.Leonard, or also called Leonard of Noblac, was born near the end of the fifth century, sometime during 496. St.Leonard made a decision to devote his life to God. King Clovis had allowed him that if a worthy prisoner was converted to Christianity, he had the right to liberate prisoners to He was born in a place called Gaul, which today would be called France. He was a Frankish noble in the court of Clovis I, the King of Franks at that time. St. Leonard died in 559, when he was 63 years old. He was venerated into the Roman Catholic churches and Anglican churches. His feast day is on November 6th.
